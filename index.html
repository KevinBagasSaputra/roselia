**Band Roselia**

Roselia adalah grup band yang bersemangat dan energik dari Akademi Otonogi yang bergengsi dalam anime "Love Live! School Idol Project". Terdiri dari empat anggota berbakat, Roselia dikenal karena lagu-lagu mereka yang menarik dan ceria yang menyebarkan kegembiraan dan kebahagiaan kepada orang-orang di sekitar mereka.

**Style**

Roselia's style is a fusion of pop, rock, and electronic elements, creating an infectious sound that gets stuck in your head. From upbeat anthems to heartfelt ballads, they have a song for every occasion. Their music videos often feature stunning visuals, clever choreography, and adorable interactions between the members.



**Impact**

As one of the most popular bands at Otonogi Academy, Roselia has inspired countless fans with their music and performances. They are a shining example of what it means to pursue your passions and make a difference through art.

Background


Roselia is an all-girl band in the BanG Dream! franchise. The group consists of five members, namely Minato Yukina on vocals, Hikawa Sayo on guitar, Imai Lisa on bass, Udagawa Ako on drums, and Shirokane Rinko on keyboard. They debuted with the song BLACK SHOUT.


A highly talented band. With their skilled performances and pro-level vocals, they are a highly praised group. Vocalist Yukina started the band with a special certain goal in mind. The other members are the like-minded Sayo (Gt.), childhood friend Lisa (Ba.), youngest and most talented Ako (Dr.), and the award-winning Rinko (Key.). They play at a well-known live house and hope someday to make it to the big time. Story
